Slowly, but surely

February 2019

Acquisition of land

Following a holiday in the island of Saint Michael, Philippe Schmitt (PT2FM) acquired a plot of land in the Parish of Brittany. 

February 2022

First tower delivered to Saint Michael

A second-hand Versatower was bought in the United Kingdom and shipped, during the pandemic, to the Azores. For ease of transportation, each of the 6m long modules had to be chopped into 3 sections.

With the ease of Covid-restrictions, delivery of the tower finally happened in February 2022. Much to our regret, the delivery took place on a stormy day, with heavy fog, rain and so much mud that the truck got stuck. 

May 2022

Planning permission filed

In May 2022, preliminary planning permission was filed with the City Council of Ponta Delgada. The matter will be under review for several weeks/months before any construction work starts.

The Echo Lodge

The Location

Located in the middle of the Atlantic Ocean, the archipelago of the Azores is one of the best hidden gems in Europe for nature tourism and radio contesting.

At 420m above sea level, the CQ8Q Echo Lodge will benefit from a low-noise environment and a clear path to North America and Continental Europe. With a steep slope towards the west, the station is only 3 km away from salt water and has been bought specifically for radio-play. 

While access to the plot can only be achieved on a 4x4, the views from up the hill along with the phenomenal radio reception make-up for the bumpy road. 

Reaching Saint Michael

The peace and quiet of the rural Parish of Brittany and its closeness to some of the most astounding landmarks in the Island of Saint Michael contribute to making the Echo Lodge the perfect location for a radio retreat.

With multiple flights a day to Lisbon and Porto, the Ponta Delgada airport also benefits from daily services to Boston and from a variety of regular flights to European capitals, including London. 

The Echo Lodge is only a 40 minute drive from the local airport of Ponta Delgada.
