Countdown to the CQWW SSB

The kick-off operation

A preliminary planning permission has now been filed with the City Council of Ponta Delgada and is now under review by the Secretary for the Environment. These proceedings are expected to take several weeks and it is therefore highly unlikely that the station will be built ahead of the CQWW SSB 2022.

As a result, an improvised warm-up operation will be arranged, most likely as a Single Op, Single Band (20m) effort.
